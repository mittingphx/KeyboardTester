Test your keyboard

After selecting your keyboard model from the dropdown, press the keys on the keyboard you want to test. Any key that is currently down will appear black, and those that have already been pressed at least one will appear dark gray. Untested keys are shown as white.

Click the Clear Keyboard button to remove any keys that you have tested.

If you cannot find your exact keyboard model, you should be able to find one that is close to your actual model. Please note that special keys, such as media keys, are not testable. Also, any key that your operating system prevents from being used in your browser will not be testable.
